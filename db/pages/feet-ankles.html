Your feet and ankles contain more than 100 bones, tendons, muscles, and ligaments, which form 33 joints, all designed to work in unison to help support and balance the weight of your body during daily activities. All it takes is a mild sprain, strain, or fracture of the foot or ankle to cause pain and inflammation, slow you down, and potentially cause greater problems if not treated correctly.

To find out more about your feet and ankles, and the injuries that can damage them, visit orthoinfo.aaos.org.
