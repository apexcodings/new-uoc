Saturday mornings from September 3 - November 5, University Orthopedics Center is conducting a Fall Sports Injury Clinic to diagnose and treat acute athletic injuries for local athletes. These free clinics are hosted by physicians and staff from our Sports Medicine Center of Excellence who are on hand to answer questions and address any immediate needs or concerns.

Clinics are scheduled Saturday mornings from 9am - 11am at 3000 Fairway Drive in Altoona and no appointments are necessary. To find out more, call (800) 505-2101 or 814-231-2101.
