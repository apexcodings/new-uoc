With NextMD, you not only access your medical record, but also a wealth of general health information online. When you log onto NextMD, you can easily view new messages from the practice or take advantage of its many powerful features as offered:

  • Access and request personal or general information.
  • Receive and review Lab Results within days of the testing.
  • Request or confirm an appointment.
  • Request medication refills.
  • Receive documents from your doctor.
  • Receive important notifications.
  • View statements and correspondence from UOC.
  • Fill out forms and previsit questionnaires requested by UOC.
