For patients needing MRI services, University Orthopedics Center offers onsite, state-of-the-art MRI technology and an experienced staff to ensure your examination is a positive experience from start to finish. An MRI exam is the safe and painless way for physicians to view detailed images of your body without exposing you to X-Ray radiation. The images are then used to help guide physicians as they diagnose your specific injury or condition.

To find out more about our MRI services, or to schedule an appointment, call 814-942-1166. All patients should bring an MRI Prescreening form to their appointment.
