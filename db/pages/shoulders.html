Your shoulders are a versatile and valuable joint. When injured, the resulting pain can significantly reduce flexibility and motion, limiting your ability to perform certain functions and adversely affecting your lifestyle. Common shoulder problems can include tendonitis, bursitis, osteoarthritis, and cartilage and muscle tears from simple overuse.
