Your neck and back are the most common areas for pain resulting from injuries or disorders. Most people will experience some form of back pain in the lumbar region — the lower back — at some point in life. Other areas of the spine that can also cause pain and discomfort are the thoracic region (middle) and cervical region (neck).

To find out more about your neck and back, and the injuries that can damage them, visit orthoinfo.aaos.org.
