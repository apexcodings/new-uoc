Whether you’re a rising high school or college star, a professional athlete, or an all-out weekend warrior, pain and injuries are never part of the game plan.

At the University Orthopedics Sports Medicine Center of Excellence, you’ll find the region’s most complete facility for sports medicine expertise and care.

The Center features on-site X-Ray and MRI services, as well as fellowship-trained sports medicine surgeons, physical therapists, certified athletic trainers, and a neuropsychologist to help diagnose and treat your sports-related injuries.

Once you’ve been evaluated and diagnosed, we work to develop an individualized treatment plan that may include nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug therapies, physical therapy and rehabilitation, injections, bracing, or surgical treatments to help keep you playing at your highest level of competition through every season.

The University Orthopedics Sports Medicine Center of Excellence is proud to support the athletes of our region and be the team physicians of choice for some of the best and brightest in the game.
