Whether you’re a rising high school or college star, a professional athlete, or an all-out weekend warrior, pain and injuries are never part of the game plan.

At University Orthopedics Sports Medicine Center of Excellence, you’ll find the region’s most comprehensive care for sports medicine expertise and care. The Center features fellowship trained sports medicine surgeons, non-surgical sports medicine physicians, on-site X-Ray and MRI services, physical therapists, certified athletic trainers, and a neuropsychologist to help diagnose and treat your sports-related injuries. Once you’ve been evaluated and diagnosed, we work to develop an individualized treatment plan that may include nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug therapies, physical therapy and rehabilitation, injections, bracing, or surgical treatments to help keep you playing at your highest level of competition through every season.
