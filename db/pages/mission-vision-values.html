Welcome To University Orthopedics Center

University Orthopedics Center (UOC) is the premier orthopedic group in the Central Pennsylvania region providing exceptional orthopedic care since 1991. Our physicians and experienced healthcare professionals, consisting of over 200 employees, care for over 125,000 patient visits each year.

UOC offers general and subspecialty orthopedic care, pain management, chiropractic and rehabilitation (physiatry) care. Additionally, the practice offers in-house MRI, digital x-ray, physical therapy, management of workers compensation, uni-dose pharmacy, clinical research and Surgery Center. Our experienced physicians are committed to returning patients to a healthy lifestyle without pain and without restrictions. The entire UOC staff thanks you, the patient, for entrusting us with your orthopedic needs.
