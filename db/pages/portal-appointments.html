The Appointment Request screen enables you to communicate with UOC online and minimizes phone calls. You can request an appointment from UOC using your NextMD Web account.

A submitted appointment request does not guarantee an actual appointment with your physician. A requested appointment must be confirmed by UOC before it is considered a booked appintment. Allow at least 24 hours to receive a reponse. If needed, you can then request a change or cancel the request.
