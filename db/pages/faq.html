Why Enroll in NextMD?

  • "Connect" with our office 24-hours a day at your convenience
  • Send and Receive messages to and from your physician
  • Request, change and cancel appointments on-line
  • Request and receive documents from our office
  • Complete forms on-line from your home or office vs filling them out in our waiting room

How do I get enrolled?

Call one of our office's today and let our staff know you would like to enroll in NextMD. You will need to provide us with a valid email address to receive your own unique NextMD token number.

Who is this for?

Any registered UOC patient with a valid email address and access to a computer with an internet connection can be enrolled.
