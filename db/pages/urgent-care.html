Injuries never happen at convenient times, which is why our Urgent Orthopedic Care Clinic offers after-hours walk-in convenience to treat acute bone and joint injuries. Using on-site X-Rays, braces and castings, our orthopedic specialists are able to treat your strain, sprain, fracture, or any other minor orthopedic injury with exceptional and timely care.

Walk-in visits are available from 5pm - 8pm Monday through Friday at our Urgent Orthopedic Care Clinic in Regent Court, State College.
