Depending on your age and lifestyle, you may be susceptible to a number of different knee problems. Osteoarthritis, the loss of cartilage in the knee joint from wear and tear, is common among baby boomers and seniors, while younger athletes often suffer from cartilage or ligament tears that can sideline them for several months.

To find out more about your knees and knee-related injuries, visit orthoinfo.aaos.org.
