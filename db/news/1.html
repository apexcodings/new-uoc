Angela W. Rowe, D.O., of Blair Orthopedics has been selected as one of the director candidates for the American Osteopathic Academy of Orthopedics. If elected, Dr. Rowe will serve as a director for a three-year term.

As a member of the AOAO Board of Directors, Dr. Rowe will work to support the mission of the American Osteopathic Academy of Orthopedics to facilitate the finest training and development of osteopathic surgeons so they are able to improve the care, healing, and quality of life for patients.

“It is an honor to be considered as a director candidate by the American Osteopathic Academy of Orthopedics,” says Dr. Rowe. “As a fellow of the AOAO, I am committed to the mission of this excellent organization.”
