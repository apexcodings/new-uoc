Background

Dr. Sensiba joined University Orthopedics Center (UOC) in August of 2010 and sees patients in need of orthopedic care at our State College office location on Regent Court. He is a fellowship trained orthopedic surgeon specializing in sports medicine. Dr. Sensiba completed his Sports Medicine Fellowship at Allegheny General Hospital, Pittsburgh, PA in July of 2010. His training and experience include unique competencies in the treatment of sports injuries, cartilage restoration, total joint replacement and minimally invasive surgery including arthroscopy of the shoulder, knee, elbow and ankle.

After receiving his Medical Degree from The Ohio State University, Columbus, Ohio in 2004, Dr. Sensiba completed his residency at the Department of Orthopedic Surgery, Wright State University, Dayton, Ohio.

Dr. Sensiba is a member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons and American Medical Association.

Dr. Sensiba resides in State College, PA with his wife Branyan. They enjoy traveling and sports.

Dr. Sensiba is currently accepting new patients at our State College office. For an appointment please call at 814-231-2101 or 1-800-505-2101.
