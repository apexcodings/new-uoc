Background

Dr. Zora is a fellowship trained Pain Management Physician. He joined University Orthopedics Center (UOC) in January of 2005 and sees patients at our State College office in the Windmere Center Building and our Altoona office located in at Station Medical Center.

Dr. Zora completed his Pain Management Fellowship at Temple University School of Medicine at the Western Pennsylvania Hospital Campus in 2002. His training and experience include unique competencies in the treatment of arthritis, back and neck pain, nerve pain, and shingles.

After receiving his Medical Degree from Midwestern University Chicago College of Osteopathic Medicine in 1994, Dr. Zora completed a rotating internship at St Francis Medical Center and his Anesthesiology Residency at The Western Pennsylvania Hospital.

Prior to joining UOC, Dr. Zora was the Co-medical Director of the Pain Management Center at Allegheny General Hospital from 2002 to 2004. During the same timeframe, he was Medical Director of The Pain Management Center, Westmorland Regional Hospital.

Dr. Zora is board certified with The American Board of Anesthesiology and The American Board of Pain Medicine. He is a member of the American Pain Society, the North American Neuromodulation Society, the American Society of Interventional Pain Physicians and the North American Spine Society.

Dr. Zora resides in Philipsburg, PA with his wife. He enjoys outdoor activities and photography.
