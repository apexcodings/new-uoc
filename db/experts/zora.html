Background

Dr. Zora is a fellowship trained, board certified Anesthesiologist and Interventional Pain Management Physician. His training and experience include unique competencies in the evaluation of arthritis, back and neck pain and nerve pain. He works as part of the spine team to evaluate and treat a wide range of spine problems. Together they offer you the best results to evaluate your problem and to develop a program to restore maximum function.

Dr. Zora was the co-Medical Director of Pain Management at Allegheny General Hospital in Pittsburgh before joining UOC. Prior to becoming a physician Dr. Zora was a practicing paramedic and worked in medical research of solid phase analytics of toxins. Dr. Zora is board certified with The American Board of Anesthesiology in both Anesthesiology and Pain Management. He is a member of the American Pain Society, the North American Neuromodulation Society, the American Society of Interventional Pain Physicians and the North American Spine Society.
