Background

Vanessa R. Colquhoun, PA-C is a board certified physician assistant who received her training at Yale University. She gained valuable experience in many areas of patient care including physical therapy and cardiac catheterization, before settling into a career in orthopedics.

Vanessa works directly with Dr. Jonathan Van Kleunen, MD in the operating room and clinic. Other duties include on call coverage, making rounds on patients in the hospital, seeing patients in the Altoona office, ordering and interpreting appropriate tests, and providing excellent care for preoperative and postoperative patients of total joint replacements.

Vanessa resides in Duncansville, PA with her husband and son.
