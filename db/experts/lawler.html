Background

Robert Lawler Jr. is an orthopedic physician assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). He graduated with Masters from King’s College in Wilkes-Barre, PA in 2016. A National Board Certified Athletic Trainer, Robert is also certified in basic life support.

Robert joined UOC in September 2016 and works out of our State College and Reedsville, PA offices. His primary responsibilities include assisting UOC physicians in surgery and completing rounds with the physicians on a daily basis. Other duties include on-call coverage and sporting event coverage.

Robert and his family reside in Millheim, PA. He enjoys outdoor activities, sporting events particularly soccer.
