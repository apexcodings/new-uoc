Background

“Efficient, timely and quality care is essential in sports medicine, and I am dedicated to making that happen.” – Dr. Varacallo

Dr. Varacallo is a sports fellowship trained board certified orthopedic physician seeing patients in need of non-surgical orthopedic care. His training and experience include unique competencies in the areas of athletic injuries, osteopathic medicine, and athletic training programs. Dr. Varacallo is dedicated to delivering appropriate, high quality care to professional and student athletes like you. Your experience with Dr. Varacallo will deliver you back to the game with a comeback your team will be ready for.

Dr. Varacallo is a member of the American Osteopathic Associations, American Academy of Family Physicians, American College of Osteopathic Family Physicians, American Medical Society for Sports Medicine, and the American College of Sports Medicine.

Dr. Varacallo resides in DuBois, PA with his wife Elissa and daughter Cecelia. He enjoys playing golf and working out.
