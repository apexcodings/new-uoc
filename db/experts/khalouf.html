Background

Dr. Khalouf joined University Orthopedics in January 2015 and sees patients in our Altoona office located at the Station Medical Center. Prior to joining UOC, Dr Khalouf spent the past twenty-five years, directing the interventional pain service at Altoona’s HealthSouth Rehabilitation Hospital where he continues to serve as President of the Medical Staff. He is well known to our practice having also worked in our State College location from 1995 until 2002.

Dr. Khalouf has previously served as Chairman of the Departments of Anesthesiology at the University of Pittsburgh’s Eye and Ear Hospital and at the Altoona General Hospital. During his time at the University of Pittsburgh, he developed and directed the first regional anesthesia and acute pain fellowship, and was the first board-certified pain physician in Blair County.

After receiving his medical degree from the Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine in May 1981, Dr. Khalouf completed an internal medicine internship at Pittsburgh’s Allegheny General Hospital and an anesthesiology residency at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania.
