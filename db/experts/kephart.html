Education

Dr. Kephart is a primary care sports medicine specialist, dedicated to diagnosing, treating and restoring people with musculoskeletal conditions back to life. He especially receives satisfaction from the successful treatment of injured athletes and seeing them then succeed on the playing field. As a competitor in triathlons, Dr. Kephart knows and understands the struggles, pain, frustration, and uncertainty that come with injuries.

Dr. Kephart is a member of the American Osteopathic Academy of Sports Medicine, the American College of Osteopathic Family Practice, the American Osteopathic Association, and the American Academy of Family Physicians. He has been instrumental in the growth of Sports Medicine care in central Pennsylvania while providing the best possible care to athletes of all ages. He has made himself available to area Athletic Trainers twenty-four hours a day in order to quickly assess and treat local high school, collegiate, and professional athletes.

Dr. Kephart resides in Hollidaysburg with his wife Lindsey and their four children. He enjoys his family, athletics, and competing in triathlons.
