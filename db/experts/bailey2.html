Background

Dr. Bailey is a board certified orthopedic surgeon who specializes in spine care. Focusing on various medical procedures such as extensive training in neurology, spinal anatomy, spinal biomechanics, and in diseases of the spine, Dr. Bailey strives to provide you with cooperative care. By working with his team in various disciplines, he provides you with efficient care for the full spectrum of spine disorders.

Dr. Bailey received his certification from the National Board of Medical Examiners in 2002, where he ranked first on the Orthopedic Surgery Written Examination. He is a Member of the American Osteopathic Association and the American Academy of Osteopathic Specialty Surgeons.

Dr. Bailey lives in State College, PA. He enjoys traveling and sporting activities.
