Background

Brad Rickabaugh is an orthopedic physician assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). He graduated from the Pennsylvania College of Technology in Health Science in Williamsport, PA in 2013, and started with UOC in August of 2013.

Brad works out of our State College, PA offices with Dr. Sensiba, and is one of UOC’s Urgent Care PA’s. His primary responsibilities include assisting UOC physicians in surgery and completing rounds with the physicians on a daily basis. Other duties include on-call coverage, and high school football game coverage.

Brad lives in State College with his wife and two daughters. On his free time he enjoys exercising and eating healthy as well as outdoor recreation activities likes hunting, fishing, boating, and hiking.
