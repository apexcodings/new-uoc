Background

Dr. Cherry is a Board Certified orthopedic surgeon who specializes in total joint replacements of the hip and knee. As the co-founder of University Orthopedics Center in 1991, his experience includes unique competencies in surgical and non-surgical orthopedics to help you regain movement.

Among his many accomplishments, Dr. Cherry won the Everett C Gordon Award for Most Outstanding Orthopedic Resident in 1990 and was editor and chairman of The Jefferson Orthopaedic Journal. He is a member of the Pennsylvania Medical Society and a Fellow with the American Academy of Orthopedic Surgery. Dr. Cherry also works as a consultant and designing surgeon for Stryker Corporation, a leader in replacement manufacturing. Dr. Cherry understands the importance of up-to-date surgical procedures for your comeback to everyday life.

Dr. Cherry has two children and resides in State College with his wife Dr. Melanie Cherry.
