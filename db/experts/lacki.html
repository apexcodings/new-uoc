Background

Andrea L. Lacki, PA-C is a board certified orthopedic physician assistant, with experience in surgery, urgent care and family practice. Andrea is self-motivated and dedicated to providing excellent medical care.

Andrea teamed up with Dr. Joshua Port, MD in October 2008 and has been assisting him in surgery and clinic since that time. In addition to assisting Dr. Port, Andrea performs history and physical exams, orders and interprets appropriate diagnostic tests, performs hospital rounds for all in-patient postoperative patients, and applies casts and splints both in the clinic and the operating room.

Andrea and her husband Marty, live in Hollidaysburg with their two children. She enjoys being a dance instructor and running.
