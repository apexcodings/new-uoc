Background

Jonathan D. Burns is an orthopedic physician assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). He graduated from Saint Francis University in Loretto, PA in 2013. Prior to joining UOC, Jonathan worked for Penn Highlands Orthopedics until they joined with UOC in 2015.

Jonathan works out of our DuBois, PA offices and has experience in fracture care and joint replacements. His primary responsibilities include assisting UOC physicians in surgery and completing rounds with the physicians on a daily basis. Other duties include on-call coverage and sporting event coverage.
