Background

Mark Maney is an orthopedic physician assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). He graduated from Lock Haven University in Lock Haven, PA in 2007. Prior to joining UOC, Mark worked Penn State Orthopedics in State College until 2013.

Mark works out of our State College, PA offices with Dr. Ellis and has experience in joint replacements. His primary responsibilities include assisting UOC physicians in surgery and completing rounds with the physicians on a daily basis. Other duties include on-call coverage.

Mark lives in State College, PA and enjoys golf, weight lifting, and volunteering as a wrestling coach.
