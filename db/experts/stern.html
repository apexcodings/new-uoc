Robin Stern is a Certified Clinical Research Coordinator at University Orthopedics Center and currently oversees clinical research trials at our office, located at 1505 9th Avenue, Altoona, PA. She received her Bachelor’s Degree in Nursing from Mount Aloysius College in 1992 while working 3 years part-time as an extern in orthopedics at Mercy Hospital, Altoona. Robin initially chose research in Oncology/Hematology clinical trials while working as a chemotherapy nurse in 2001, and has since worked with research in the areas of Oncology, Hematology, CHF, Diabetes, Orthopedics and Academia.

Robin shares UOC’s position that clinical trials are an important research tool for advancing medical knowledge and patient care.
