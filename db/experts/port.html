Dr. Port is a fellowship-trained and board-certified orthopedic surgeon, focused on sports medicine and knee and shoulder surgery. Through extensive training and continuing medical education, he is committed to providing the most expert, most compassionate, most minimally invasive, and most current care to patients, like you, in need of orthopedic care.

Dr. Port is a member of the American Academy of Orthopedic Surgeons, the Pennsylvania Orthopedic Society, the American Orthopedic Society of Sports Medicine, the Council of Delegates, the Arthroscopy Association of North American, the Communication & Health Policy Committees, the Mid-Atlantic Shoulder & Elbow Society, and the Membership Chair for Pennsylvania. He has established a loyal patient base in Pennsylvania by accurately diagnosing the scope of your injury in a timely fashion. As a member of the sports community, he enjoys working with you to define the best solution for your injury through a combination of time, medication, exercise, limitation of activity, or surgery in order to get you back into your routine.

Dr. Port resides in Altoona with his wife Leeanne and their four children. He enjoys athletic activities with his family, including cycling, running, and hiking.
