Background

David S. Claar, PA-C is a board certified physician assistant with a strong interest in sports medicine. He is a certified health fitness specialist and enjoys seeing new patients in the clinic and diagnosing various issues of the knee, shoulder and elbow.

In 2012, David had the opportunity to work with Dr. Joshua Port, MD and has been assisting him in the operating room and clinic ever since. In addition, he performs multiple office procedures including knee and shoulder injections, knee and bursal aspirations, and ultrasound guided injections. Other duties include rounding, call coverage and discharge summaries.

David and his wife reside in Hollidaysburg, PA with their son. He enjoys all areas of fitness instruction and training.
