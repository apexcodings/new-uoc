Background

Dr. Ellis joined University Orthopedics Center (UOC) in September 1994 and sees patients in need of orthopedic care at both our State College and Altoona offices. He is a sports medicine fellowship trained and board certified orthopedic surgeon. Dr Ellis completed his Sports Medicine Fellowship at The Milton S. Hershey Medical Center. His training and experience include unique competencies in the areas of total knee replacement, shoulder, and sports injuries.

After receiving his B.S. in biology from The Pennsylvania State University in 1984, Dr. Ellis received his medical degree from The Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine in 1988. He completed his internship and his residency at the University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey, Kennedy Memorial Hospital, in 1993.

Dr. Ellis is a member of the American Medical Association, American Osteopathic Association, Pennsylvania Medical Society, American Osteopathic Medical Association, American Osteopathic Academy of Orthopedic Surgeons, Phi Sigma Gamma Medical Fraternity, Arthroscopy Association of North America, and American Osteopathic Board of Orthopedic Surgery – Sports Medicine, CAQ Committee.

Dr. Ellis resides in Boalsburg, PA with his wife and 3 children. He enjoys traveling, sports, and working out.

Dr. Ellis sees patients in our State College and Altoona offices. For an appointment please call 814-231-2101, 814-949-4050, or 1-800-505-2101.
