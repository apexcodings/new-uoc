Background

Michael Reichert is an orthopedic physician assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). He graduated from Lemoyne College in Syracuse, NY in 2000. Prior to joining UOC, Michael worked for the Orthopedic Pennsylvania Rochester General Hospital in New York.

Michael works out of our State College, PA offices with Dr. Bailey, and has experience in spine care. His primary responsibilities include assisting UOC physicians in surgery and completing rounds with the physicians on a daily basis. Other duties include on-call coverage.
