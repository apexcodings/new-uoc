Background

David Vanchure is an orthopedic physician assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). He graduated from Kings College in Wilkes-Barre, PA in 2001. Prior to joining UOC, David worked for Penn Highlands Orthopedics until they joined with UOC in 2015.

David works out of our DuBois, PA office with Dr. Torretti and Dr. Barter, and has experience orthopedic trauma and spine. His primary responsibilities include assisting UOC physicians in surgery and completing rounds with the physicians on a daily basis. Other duties include on-call coverage.

David lives in Treasure Lake, DuBois, PA with his wife Erin and two daughters. In his free time he enjoys fishing, golfing, and going to Pittsburgh Pirates games.
