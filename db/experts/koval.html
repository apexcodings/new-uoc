Background

Leah Koval is an orthopedic physician assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). She graduated from Duquesne University in Pittsburgh, PA in 2006. Prior to joining UOC, Leah worked for Urgent Care: MedExpress until 2008.

Leah works out of our State College, PA offices and has experience in hands and upper extremities. Her primary responsibilities include assisting UOC physicians in surgery and completing rounds with the physicians on a daily basis. Other duties include on-call coverage and sporting event coverage.
