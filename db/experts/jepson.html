Sue Jepson is the Certified Clinical Research Coordinator at UOC and is currently overseeing multiple clinical trials. Clinical research is performed at research sites all over the world in order to evaluate new pharmaceutical medications and/or new devices or procedures not yet approved by the FDA. The FDA mandates and monitors the research sites to follow strict guidelines and protocols.

Sue is a Registered Nurse who specializes in Clinical Research. She attended Pennsylvania State University, graduating in January 1983 with a degree in Nursing. She began working in the area of research in 1994, after functioning as an Intensive Care Nurse for 14 years. Sue lives in Centre Hall with her husband and four children.
