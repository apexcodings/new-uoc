While completing my residency, I gained experience covering teams at sporting events, such as the Philadelphia International Cycling Championship and the Air Force Cycling Classic.

As an athlete myself, I understand how important it is to be able to perform in a sport or activity in peak condition. To keep in good physical health, I enjoy racing for the District of Columbia Velo cycling team.
