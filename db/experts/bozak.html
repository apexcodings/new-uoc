Background

“I chose the specialty of physiatry because the focus is on function and quality of life, and I understand the importance of being able to do the things you love. ” – Dr. Bozak

Dr. Bozak is a Physiatrist who specializes in providing nonsurgical treatment for injury or illness to decrease pain and restore function of the nerves, muscles, and bones. A physiatrist has many tools to help accomplish this objective including medicines, therapies, various injections, and other modalities. It is his goal to make you feel better so you can return to the activities you love.

Dr. Bozak is a member of the American Academy of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation, Mensa (International High IQ Society), and the United States Cycling Federation. As a member of the District of Columbia Velo cycling team, he understands how important it is to be able to perform in a sport or activity in peak condition.

Dr. Bozak resides in Hollidaysburg with his wife Andrea and their two daughters. He is an avid bike racer, Crossfit competitor, skier, and scuba diver.
