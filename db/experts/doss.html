Background

Dr. Doss is a foot and ankle surgeon who specializes in the surgical and conservative treatment of foot, ankle and lower leg conditions in children and adults. He treats diabetic foot deformities and helps people with diabetes avoid amputation by preserving their limbs. He also treats ankle disorders, athletic injuries, ankle and foot arthritis, flatfoot, bunions, hammer toes, peripheral neuropathy and Charcot foot deformity.

Dr. Doss has extensive training in Charcot foot deformity correction, circular external fixation for deformity correction and other complex foot and ankle disorders. Dr. Doss is actively involved in clinical research and focuses on continual education in the latest foot and ankle treatments. All it takes is a mild sprain, strain, or fracture of the foot or ankle to cause pain, slow you down, and potentially cause more problems. Dr. Doss can help you recover from your foot and ankle pain.

In his free time, Dr. Doss enjoys mountain biking, hiking, weight lifting and cycling.
