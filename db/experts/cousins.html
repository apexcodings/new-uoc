Background

Dr. Cousins is a pain management specialist is a fellowship trained interventional pain management physician. His training and experience include unique competencies in the treatment of arthritis, back and neck pain, nerve pain, and shingles. Dr. Cousins understands that not all patients have the opportunity for surgery, and is ready to assist you in your comeback to easing pain.

Dr. Cousins is board certified from the American Board of Anesthesiology and the American Board of Pain Medicine. He is a member of the American Society of Anesthesiologists, the International Spinal Injection Society, the American Society of Interventional Pain Physicians, and the Pennsylvania Society of Anesthesiologists. Practicing for twenty two years in the area, Dr. Cousins can guarantee you an expert approach in pain management.

Dr. Cousins lives with his wife and children in Lemont, PA. He enjoys running and coaching his children’s sports activities.
