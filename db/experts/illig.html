Background

Ms. Jennifer Illig is an Orthopedic Physician Assistant at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). She graduated from Saint Francis University, Loretto, PA in 2006. Jennifer joined UOC in June 2006 and works out of our State College and Altoona offices.

Jennifer assists Dr. Kenneth L. Cherry in the operating room. She also assists Dr. Cherry in seeing patients at our State College Windmere Centre office. Other duties include making rounds on patients in the hospital, on call coverage, discharge summaries, andx-ray interpretation.

Jennifer resides in State College, PA.
