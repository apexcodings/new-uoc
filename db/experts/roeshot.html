Background

Dr. Roeshot co-founded University Orthopedics Center in 1991. He is a board certified orthopedic surgeon and sees patients in need of orthopedic care at our State College office located on Regent Court. His training and experience include unique competencies in knee replacement, hip replacement, hand/wrist, shoulder, and sports injuries.

After graduating from The Pennsylvania State University in 1982, Dr. Roeshot earned his medical doctorate from Temple University School of Medicine in 1986. He completed his orthopedic residency at Geisinger Medical Center in 1991.

Dr. Roeshot is an active member of the Pennsylvania Medical Society, the Eastern Orthopaedic Association, and a Fellow Member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgery.

Dr. Roeshot has two children and resides in State College, PA with his wife.

Dr. Roeshot sees patients in our State College office. If you would like to schedule an appointment please call 814-231-2101 or 800-505-2101.
