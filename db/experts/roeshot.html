Background

Dr. Roeshot is a Board Certified orthopedic surgeon who specializes in total joint replacements of the hip, knee, shoulder, and wrist. As the co-founder of University Orthopedics Center in 1991, his experience includes unique competencies in surgical and non-surgical orthopedics to help gain mobility through an expansive approach for his patients, like you.

Dr. Roeshot is an active member of the Pennsylvania Medical Society, the Eastern Orthopaedic Association, and a Fellow Member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgery. He has been seeing patients for twenty two years in the Centre County and surrounding areas. Dr. Roeshot’s research and developments focus on you regaining strength to return to your active daily lives.

Dr. Roeshot has two children and resides in State College, PA with his wife.
