“Let’s work together to make you better!” – Dr. Rowe

Dr. Rowe is a board certified and fellowship trained orthopedic surgeon committed to providing you with the best up-to-date surgical and nonsurgical treatment for your injuries. As the first female orthopedic surgeon in Blair County, she firmly believes that getting better is a collaborative effort between the patient and the physician. Let her know your problems, concerns, and expectations and she will work with you to succeed in making your life better.

Dr. Rowe is on the Board of Directors of the American Osteopathic Academy of Orthopedics. She is also Second Vice President of the Pennsylvania Orthopedic Society and on the Special Leadership Council for the Pennsylvania Medical Society. At the local level, she serves on the Medical Executive Committee at Nason Hospital and as the Chief Medical Officer at the Advanced Center for Surgery.

As the first female in her orthopedic residency training program, Dr. Rowe has always been at the forefront in her career in medicine.

Dr. Rowe resides in Hollidaysburg with her husband Paul and their son. She enjoys fishing, gourmet cooking, gardening and supporting her son’s hockey team.
