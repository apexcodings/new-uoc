Background

Erin is a certified orthopedic physician assistant (PA-C) at University Orthopedics Center (UOC). She received his Master’s Degree in Health Sciences from Lock Haven University, Lock Haven, PA in 2011 and received her NCCPA (National Commission on Certification of Physician Assistants) in June 2011. In 2006, Erin received her Bachelors of Science degree in Athletic Training at The Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA.

Prior to joining UOC, Erin worked as a PA-C at Moshannon Valley Orthopedics.

Erin currently assists Dr. Christopher J. Lincoski in the operating room. Other duties include seeing patients at UOC’s Regent Court office located in State College, PA, and UOC’s office located in Altoona, PA., making rounds on patients in the hospital, on-call coverage, discharge summaries and x-ray interpretation.

Erin currently resides in Julian, PA.
