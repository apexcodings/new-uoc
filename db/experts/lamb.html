Background

Dr. Lamb joined University Orthopedics Center (UOC) in November of 2004 and sees patients in need of chiropractic care at our State College office. His training and experience include unique competencies in chiropractic and adjunctive therapy treatment. Dr. Lamb is an integral part of UOCâ€™s Spine Team.

After receiving his Bachelor of Science degree at Indiana University of PA, Dr. Lamb received his Doctorate of Chiropractic at Palmer College of Chiropractic in 1998. Upon graduating, he founded and opened Lamb Chiropractic in Philipsburg, PA. Dr. Lamb practiced chiropractics at both Lamb Chiropractic and Nittany Valley Chiropractic from 1999-2004 before joining UOC.

Dr. Lamb is a member of the Pennsylvania Chiropractic Association and the Moshannon Valley Economic Development Partnership.

Residing in Philipsburg, PA with his wife and children, Dr. Lamb enjoys outdoor activities and spending time with his family.

Dr. Lamb is currently accepting new patients in our State College office located at Windmere Center. For an appointment please call at 814-949-4050 or 1-800-505-2101.
