Background

Dr. Lamb sees patients in need of chiropractic care at our State College office. His training and experience include unique competencies in chiropractic and adjunctive therapy treatment. Dr. Lamb is an integral part of UOC’s Spine Team. The neck and back are the most common sources of pain in our bodies. Most people will experience back pain in the lumbar region—the lower back—at some point in their lives. Dr. Lamb is available to offer you nonsurgical treatment for neck and back pain, injuries, and disorders.

Dr. Lamb is a member of the Pennsylvania Chiropractic Association and the Moshannon Valley Economic Development Partnership.

Residing in Philipsburg, PA with his wife and children, Dr. Lamb enjoys outdoor activities and spending time with his family.
