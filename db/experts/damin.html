Background

Anthony C. Damin, PA-C is a board certified physician assistant, with advanced skills in orthopedic surgery and an outgoing personality. He is an independent clinician who is committed to providing exceptional care as an integral member of the UOC health care team.

Anthony has been assisting Dr. Robert Singer, DO in clinic and the operating room since July 2011. His responsibilities include the evaluation and treatment of new patients, consultations, preoperative and postoperative patient management, and rounding. Anthony is experienced in all areas of general orthopedic surgery and his skills include advanced knowledge of injections to the knee, shoulder, wrist and elbow.

Anthony resides in Hollidaysburg, PA
