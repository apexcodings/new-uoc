Background

Dr. Barter is a Board Certified Fellowship Trained orthopedic surgeon committed to providing you with the best up-to-date surgical and nonsurgical treatments for your orthopedic needs. His training and experience include unique competencies in the treatment of foot and ankle, knee, hand, trauma and pediatrics. While foot and ankle are his specialty, his wide array of research and focus helps make your steps to recovery a comeback to remember.

Dr. Barter is a member of the American Osteopathic Association of Orthopaedic Surgery, American Academy of Orthopedic Surgeons, American Osteopathic Association, Pennsylvania Osteopathic Medical Association, and the American Orthopedic Foot and Ankle Society.

Dr. Barter lives in State College, PA with his kids. He enjoys traveling and boating.
