Background

Dr. McClellan is a specialist in general orthopedics and is a board certified orthopedic surgeon. His training and experience include unique competencies in the areas of Total Joint Replacement, Fractures, and Sports Medicine. Dr. McClellan strives to collaboratively work with his team for you to develop a full recovery. With recent technological advancements in both surgical and nonsurgical treatment, he is able to take a less invasive approach when addressing your conditions. The opportunity to help heal you and relieve your aches and pains is of upmost importance to him.

Dr. McClellan is a member of the American Association of Hip/Knee Surgeons, the American Academy of Orthopedic Surgeons, the American Osteopathic Academy of Orthopedics, the American Medical Association, the American Osteopathic Association, and the Pennsylvania Orthopedic Society.

Dr. McClellan resides in Altoona, PA, with his wife and children. He enjoys running, basketball, weightlifting, movies, sporting events, and traveling.
